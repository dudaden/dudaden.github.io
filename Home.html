Welcome to TOURISM PROVIDERS

In this project we would like to show the unusual places of Russia for the for foreign travelers. Also will be information about cities, but we would like to focus on unique places.
For now only few places will be on the website, but after we would like to provide more and even about different countries. People prefer to travel to Turkey or Egypt, or even to Iceland or Norway, instead of traveling to Russia. But she is very large, beautiful and diverse. There is something to see here. Why don't people travel in Russia? There are many reasons. The most obvious is the infrastructure. It’s very difficult and expensive to get to most interesting places, there is no place to stop, no place to eat, tickets are expensive, there are no parking lots, public transport is not developed, in a word, there are many objective reasons. But there are others. For example, the complete absence on the Internet of systematic comprehensive information about Russia. Lack of well-established images of cities. Therefore, we want to change this opinion in people's heads and show the beauty and charm of this great country.

Tourism is a trip made by a person in his spare time from his main job for recreational, educational, professional, business, sports, religious and other purposes. This is one of the types of outdoor activities, the best way to escape from the hustle, see new and interesting, gain positive emotions.
Tourism provides an opportunity to get acquainted with the culture of other countries and regions, satisfies the curiosity of a person, enriches him spiritually, physically heals, promotes the development of personality. It allows you to combine relaxation with new knowledge.
